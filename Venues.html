Valetta

Valetta and its stunning views provide the perfect venue for civil weddings, ceremonies and receptions. Valetta is available on an exclusive basis only, which ensures that your day is truly unique and memorable. Up to 100 guests can be accommodated for wedding breakfast.

Hillsborough Castle

A oasis of calm on the edge of the city of Belfast, Hillsborough Castle nestles within 98 acres of landscaped gardens. The Throne room, State Dining Room, Red Room, Lady Greys and the Drawing Room are licensed for weddings and civil partnerships..

The Gables

The Gambles at Kensington Palace is one of the most uniquely stylish wedding venues in west London. Licensed as a civil partnership and wedding ceremony venue, The Gambles offers a glamorous and cosmopolitan setting to host your special day.

Oaklands

Oaklands is a true sanctuary with over 100 acres of breath-taking views of Nottinghamshire. Our beautiful grounds, exclusive rooms and unique setting will ensure a wedding to remember. We deliver weddings with a passion that comes from understanding our couples’ individual requirements. Commitment to providing unrivalled quality with our experienced team means we bring inspiration and dedication to ensure every wedding is unique.

Orchard Cottage

Your wedding day should be unforgettable; an experience to remember for you and all of your guests. A wedding at Orchard Cottage will be just that – the perfect day in the perfect venue. Whether you choose Oakland Cottage for your wedding reception or the full civil wedding or civil partnership ceremony, we can tailor a package that is just as individual as you are, creating a special day that is everything you imagined.

Pichard Palace

Pichard Palace offers the perfect wedding venue and everything you need for a memorable day. Our private function room with breathtaking views can seat up to 420 people without a dancefloor, 300 guests with a dancefloor. We can have more guests for evening receptions where formal dining is not required, up to 200 guests. The fantastic setting can also be enjoyed from the patio area next to our lake. Capture your special day with photographs around the lake and grounds.
